The Beauty of Tulip Flowers

Tulips are one of my favourite flowers because of their elegant shape, vibrant colours, and the sense of calm they bring to any garden. Originating from Central Asia and later becoming iconic in the Netherlands, tulips are now grown and loved all around the world. They bloom in early spring and come in hundreds of varieties, each with its own charm. Whether displayed in a vase or planted in a garden bed, tulips always add a touch of beauty and freshness to their surroundings.

Why I Like Tulips

  • They come in many bright and beautiful colours.
  • They symbolise love, happiness, and new beginnings.
  • They are easy to grow and care for in spring.
